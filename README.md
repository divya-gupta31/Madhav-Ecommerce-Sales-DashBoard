# Madhav-Ecommerce-Sales-DashBoard
📊 1. Project Title

Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI Data Analytics Project

📝 2. Short Description

This interactive dashboard analyzes ecommerce sales performance across different states, customers, categories, and time periods. It helps businesses track revenue, profit trends, and customer behavior to make data-driven decisions.

🛠️ 3. Tech Stack
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation
Excel / CSV – Data storage and preprocessing
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculations & measures
Data Cleaning Techniques – Handling missing/duplicate values
📂 4. Data Source
Sales and Orders data (CSV files)
Contains information like:
Customer names
States
Order amount
Profit
Quantity
Payment modes
Product categories
🚀 5. Features / Highlights

🔴 Business Problem
Ecommerce businesses often struggle to track performance across multiple dimensions like sales, profit, customer behavior, and payment preferences in one place.

🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:-

Tracks overall sales and profit performance
Identifies top customers and regions
Analyzes category-wise demand
Monitors monthly profit trends
Helps in improving marketing and sales strategies

📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
KPI Cards (Top Section)
Total Amount, Profit, Quantity, and AOV give a quick overview of business performance
Profit by Month (Bar Chart)
Shows monthly fluctuations in profit (identifying loss & peak months)
Amount by State
Highlights which state contributes the most revenue
Amount by Customer Name
Identifies top customers driving sales
Quantity by Category (Donut Chart)
Shows which product category is most popular (Clothing dominates)
Quantity by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)
Reveals customer payment preferences (UPI, COD, Debit Card, EMI)
Profit by Sub-Category
Shows which products generate maximum profit

📈 Business Impact & Insights
Marketing Optimization:-

Focus more on high-performing categories like Clothing to maximize revenue
Customer Targeting:
Identify top customers and create loyalty or retention strategies
Regional Strategy:
Invest more in high-performing states like Sikkim
Profit Improvement:
Analyze loss-making months and optimize pricing or costs
Payment Insights:
Promote popular payment modes (like UPI) for better conversion

💡 Conclusion:-
This dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, helping businesses make smarter decisions, increase profitability, and optimize operations.
